About Us

Dilpreet’s Music Store is a rising music retailer specializing in premium vinyl records featuring your favorite artists and albums. Founded and owned by Dilpreet—also known as Dilron James—our mission is to deliver high-quality records quickly and affordably. While we currently operate exclusively within the United States, we’re planning to expand our online presence and open international locations in the near future. For inquiries or more information, feel free to contact us.
